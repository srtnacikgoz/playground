# BONBON-COLOR-GRADIENT

Horizontal arrangement showcasing natural color spectrum from uploaded bonbons.

---

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional product photography of SIX bonbons arranged in horizontal line showing color gradient. Maintain each bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded images. Arrange left to right in color sequence as uploaded (dark to light or vice versa). Top-down 90-degree angle. Even 5-6cm spacing between pieces. Standard backdrop: soft off-white (RGB 250,248,245) with 3% subtle texture. Even studio lighting, consistent across all, high-key. Visual color story flow. 8k photorealistic.
```

---

## Konsept

**Ne yapar:** Altı bonbon'u renk geçişi sırasında yatay çizgide sunar.

**Anahtar Özellikler:**
- 6 bonbon yatay sıra
- Renk geçişi (koyu→açık veya tersi)
- Top-down 90° açı
- 5-6cm eşit aralık
- Standart backdrop
