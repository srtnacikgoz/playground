# BONBON-STANDART

Clean, appetizing hero shot - the essential bonbon photograph.

---

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional food photography of single bonbon, maximum clarity and appetite appeal. Maintain bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded image. Bonbon resting flat on surface, slightly angled (15°) to show both top decoration and side profile. Shot from 35-degree elevated angle - the "food photography sweet spot" showing depth and form. Crisp sharp focus on entire bonbon, every detail visible. Soft directional light from 45° front-left creating gentle highlight on coating and subtle shadow for dimension. Standard backdrop: soft off-white (RGB 250,248,245) with 3% subtle texture. Warm inviting atmosphere, makes viewer want to eat it. 8k photorealistic.
```

---

## Konsept

**Ne yapar:** Bonbon'u en sade, en net, en iştah açıcı şekilde gösterir.

**Anahtar Özellikler:**
- Tek bonbon, yüzeyde (levitation yok)
- 15° hafif açı (üst + yan görünür)
- 35° kamera açısı (food photography klasiği)
- Tüm detaylar keskin fokus
- Işık: 45° ön-sol, yumuşak, boyut veren gölge
- Standart backdrop
- **Amaç:** Yeme isteği uyandırmak

**Ne zaman kullanılır:**
- E-ticaret ana görsel
- Menü/katalog
- Sosyal medya temel paylaşım
- Her durumda "güvenli" seçim
