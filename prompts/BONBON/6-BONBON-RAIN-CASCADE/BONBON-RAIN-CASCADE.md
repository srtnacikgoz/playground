# BONBON-RAIN-CASCADE

Dynamic cascade of bonbons falling from above, frozen mid-motion.

---

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional action product photography of SIX bonbons cascading downward in frozen mid-fall moment. Maintain each bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded images. Bonbons at various heights (15-5cm) creating vertical cascade. Top bonbons smaller (perspective), bottom larger. Front 30-degree angle. Subtle motion blur on highest, sharp on lower. Realistic shadows indicating height. One bonbon just touching surface. Standard backdrop: soft off-white (RGB 250,248,245) with 3% subtle texture. High-speed photography lighting. 8k photorealistic.
```

## Konsept

**Ne yapar:** Altı bonbon'u düşme anında dondurulmuş cascade efektiyle sunar.

**Anahtar Özellikler:**
- 6 bonbon düşen cascade
- Farklı yükseklikler (15-5cm)
- Perspektif: üsttekiler küçük, alttakiler büyük
- Hafif motion blur (üstte), keskin fokus (altta)
- Bir bonbon yüzeye değiyor
- Standart backdrop
