Central bonbon surrounded by delicate dust cloud burst matching bonbon's color.

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional product photography of bonbon floating with delicate dust burst. Maintain bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded image. Bonbon centered, sharp and glossy as hero - clearly visible, NOT obscured by dust. SUBTLE organic dust cloud around bonbon - dust color matching bonbon's natural tones (cocoa for dark, gold for caramel, pink for ruby, etc.). Asymmetric irregular cloud shape, NEVER circular ring.
```
