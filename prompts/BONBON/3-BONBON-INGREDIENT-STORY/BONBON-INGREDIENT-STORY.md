# BONBON-INGREDIENT-STORY

Bonbons with raw ingredients showcasing origin and quality transparency.

---

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional editorial product photography of THREE bonbons arranged with raw premium ingredients. Maintain each bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded images. Loose triangular bonbon arrangement. Surrounding ingredients: cacao pod (halved), roasted hazelnuts, gold leaf, cocoa nibs, vanilla bean - colors matching bonbon tones where applicable. 45-degree elevated angle. Standard backdrop: soft off-white (RGB 250,248,245) with 3% subtle texture. Soft natural studio lighting, high-key. Farm-to-chocolate story. 8k photorealistic.
```

## Konsept

**Ne yapar:** Ham malzemelerle çikolatanın hikayesini anlatır.

**Anahtar Özellikler:**
- 3 bonbon üçgen dizilim
- Çevreleyen malzemeler: kakao, fındık, gold leaf, nibs, vanilya
- 45° açı, flat-lay storytelling
- Tümü yüzeyde
- Standart backdrop

**Renk Kuralı:** Malzemelerin doğal renkleri korunur (kakao kahverengi, fındık doğal vb.), ama ortam ışığı bonbon tonlarına uyumlu.
