# BONBON-SHADOW-PLAY

Dramatic directional lighting creating strong geometric shadow patterns.

---

## MAIN PROMPT

```
Professional artistic product photography of bonbon with dramatic geometric shadow. Maintain bonbon's exact shape, coating, color, and decorations from uploaded image. Bonbon positioned left-of-center, strong directional light from 75° side creating elongated bold shadow extending right. Shadow becomes design element from bonbon's actual form. Top-down 90-degree angle. Standard backdrop: soft off-white (RGB 250,248,245) with 3% subtle texture. Film noir elegance, light/shadow interplay. 8k photorealistic.
```
