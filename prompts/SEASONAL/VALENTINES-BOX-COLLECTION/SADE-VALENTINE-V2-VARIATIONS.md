## V2.1 - HIERARCHY (Kalp Hiyerarşisi)

Kalplere "ana + eşlik edenler" hiyerarşisi eklendi.

```
Product photography of a rectangular white matte gift box on soft cream background.

BOX SPECIFICATIONS:
- Rectangular lid box, landscape orientation
- Matte white cardboard with soft-touch finish
- Box rotated 25 degrees to the right
- Subtle shadow beneath box

RED LINE ART ELEMENTS (dark red #B22234, thin outline, hand-drawn feel):

1. LEFT HEART GROUP (lighter, supporting):
   - Position: Upper left corner of lid
   - 3 hearts total (NOT 4)
   - 2 small hearts (0.6cm) + 1 medium heart (1cm)
   - More sparse arrangement, breathing space between
   - Delicate, secondary role

2. RIGHT HEART GROUP (hero, main focus):
   - Position: Right side, center-lower area
   - 3 hearts total (NOT 4)
   - 1 LARGE hero heart (2.5cm) as focal point
   - 2 small hearts (0.8cm) accompanying the large one
   - "One main heart with companions" hierarchy
   - Large heart slightly tilted for organic feel

3. TEXT:
   - "Sade Chocolate" in thin elegant serif font
   - "Sade" normal weight, "Chocolate" slightly lighter/thinner
   - Subtle letter-spacing (tracking) increased 5%
   - Position: Center-left, slightly above vertical center

4. DIAGONAL LINE:
   - Simple straight diagonal line
   - Starts from upper right corner, goes down-left
   - Length: 4-5cm, Width: 0.7cm
   - Same dark red, solid fill

BACKGROUND:
- Soft cream backdrop (RGB 250, 248, 245)
- Subtle paper texture
- Soft diffused lighting

STYLE: Minimal Valentine, heart hierarchy design, editorial luxury, 8K resolution
```

```
Product photography of a rectangular white matte gift box on soft cream background.

BOX SPECIFICATIONS:
- Rectangular lid box, landscape orientation

- Box rotated 25 degrees to the right
- Subtle shadow beneath box

RED LINE ART ELEMENTS (dark red #B22234):

1. LEFT HEART GROUP:
   - Position: Upper left corner
   - 3 hearts: 2 small (0.6cm) + 1 medium (1cm)
   - Sparse, breathing space

2. RIGHT HEART GROUP:
   - Position: Right center-lower
   - 3 hearts: 1 large hero (2.5cm) + 2 small (0.8cm)
   - Hierarchy composition

3. TEXT:
   - "Sade Chocolate" elegant serif
   - Slight tracking increase
   - Center-left position

4. DIAGONAL LINE WITH HEART KNOTS ("Connection" metaphor):
   - Diagonal line from upper right to lower left
   - Length: 4-5cm, Width: 0.5cm (slightly thinner)

BACKGROUND:
- Soft cream backdrop (RGB 250, 248, 245)

- Soft lighting
```

## V2.4 - EMBOSSED ELEGANCE (Dokunsal Kalite)

Aynı tasarım ama emboss/deboss efektleriyle - "dokunarak hisset" trendi.

```
Product photography of a rectangular white matte gift box on soft cream background.

BOX SPECIFICATIONS:
- Rectangular lid box, landscape orientation
- TEXTURED white cardboard (linen or cotton paper texture visible)
- Soft-touch matte finish
- Box rotated 25 degrees to the right
- Side lighting to emphasize texture

EMBOSSED/DEBOSSED ELEMENTS:

1. HEARTS (BLIND EMBOSS - raised, no ink):
   - Left group: 3 hearts embossed (raised from surface)
   - Right group: 3 hearts embossed (raised)
   - NO COLOR - same white as box, only texture
   - Shadows from side lighting reveal the embossed shapes
   - Tactile, touchable quality

2. TEXT (DEBOSSED + RED INK):
   - "Sade Chocolate" pressed INTO surface (debossed)
   - Filled with dark red ink (#B22234)
   - Creates depth, tactile feel
   - Center-left position

3. DIAGONAL LINE (EMBOSSED + RED):
   - Raised stripe (embossed)
   - Filled with dark red
   - Width: 0.7cm
   - Tactile stripe you can feel

TEXTURE EMPHASIS:
- Strong side lighting (60° angle) to show emboss shadows
- Paper texture visible on box surface
- "Touch and feel" premium quality

BACKGROUND:
- Soft cream backdrop
- Directional lighting emphasizing textures
- Editorial product photography

STYLE: Tactile luxury, embossed elegance, quiet luxury trend, premium Valentine, 8K
```

## V2.5 - INNER SURPRISE (İç Kapak Sürprizi)

Dış minimal, iç kapakta büyük kalp sürprizi - "açınca wow" efekti.

```
Product photography of premium chocolate gift box with lid PARTIALLY OPEN revealing inside.

BOX SPECIFICATIONS:
- Rectangular lid box, landscape orientation
- EXTERIOR: Matte white, minimal design
- Lid lifted at 45° angle, showing interior
- Box rotated 20 degrees

EXTERIOR ELEMENTS (minimal):
- Only "Sade Chocolate" text in dark red, center
- Small single heart (1cm) next to text
- NO diagonal line on exterior
- Ultra minimal, clean

INTERIOR SURPRISE (revealed by open lid):
- Inside of lid: Deep burgundy/wine red color (#722F37)
- LARGE single line-art heart (8cm) in cream/gold
- Heart centered on inner lid
- Continuous single-stroke drawing style
- "Wow" moment when opening

COMPOSITION:
- Box exterior visible (minimal white)
- Lid open showing burgundy interior with large heart
- Contrast between minimal outside and rich inside
- Anticipation and surprise storytelling

BACKGROUND:
- Soft cream backdrop
- Soft lighting
- Gift unveiling moment aesthetic

STYLE: Surprise unboxing, interior reveal, romantic luxury, Instagram moment, 8K
```
