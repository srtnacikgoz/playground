## VARYASYON: Direct on Surface

```
Using uploaded image(s) as reference for the pastry.

9:16 vertical composition. [PASTRY] placed directly on white marble surface - no plate. Positioned off-center in lower portion of frame. The marble's subtle natural veining provides the only texture.

Vast emptiness surrounds the pastry. Off-white backdrop merges with white marble at horizon.

Extremely soft, diffused lighting. Minimal shadow - just enough to ground the pastry. No props of any kind.

Raw, confident minimalism. The pastry exists alone, complete, needing nothing.

8k quality.

--no plate, props, utensils, decorations, strong shadows, busy background
```

---

## VARYASYON: Floating Void

```
Using uploaded image(s) as reference for the pastry.

9:16 vertical composition. [PASTRY] appearing to float in seamless off-white void. No visible surface, no horizon line - pure continuous tone surrounding the pastry.

Positioned in lower third, the pastry casts only the softest shadow below (or no shadow for complete float).

Light comes from everywhere and nowhere - completely diffused, no direction. The pastry is a golden object in infinite quiet space.

Maximum editorial restraint. Product as art object. Total focus, zero distraction.

8k quality.

--no visible surface, horizon line, shadows, reflections, props, anything except pastry
```

## KRİTİK KURALLAR

1. **Hiçbir prop ekleme** - tek bir çatal bile kompozisyonu bozar
2. **Negatif alan cesur olsun** - frame'in %60-70'i boş kalabilir
3. **Renk sadece üründen** - backdrop ve yüzey nötr kalmalı
4. **Gölge minimal** - var ama dikkat çekmeyen
